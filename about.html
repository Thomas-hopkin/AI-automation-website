About

Hi, I'm Tom — I think most software is over-sold and under-explained.

TH Automations exists to fix the repetitive, manual parts of running a service business — without asking you to become a tech person to get there.

Why I started this

Most service businesses — clinics, agencies, contractors, consultancies — run on the same handful of repetitive tasks: intake forms, reminders, follow-ups, status updates, reports. Those tasks don't need a talented person doing them by hand. They need a system.

I got tired of watching skilled teams spend hours a week on work a system could do in seconds, and of watching "automation" get sold as complicated software that nobody on the team actually understood or trusted.

How I'm different

I explain everything in plain language before I build it. If I can't explain what a system does and why, in a sentence you'd say to a colleague, I haven't done my job yet.

I build with the tools you already use where I can, so your team isn't learning a new platform on top of everything else. And I stay honest about when automation isn't the right answer.

What I care about

The principles behind every project.

01

Plain language, always

No jargon walls. If a system's logic can't be explained simply, it's not ready to ship.

02

Built around you

I fit the system to your process — not the other way around.

03

Honest recommendations

If automation isn't worth it for a task, I'll tell you — even if that means a smaller project.
