Services

Systems built around how you already work.

I don't sell software you have to learn. I build automation into the tools you already use, so your team's day looks the same — minus the repetitive parts.

Client intake & onboarding

A new lead fills out a form, books a call, or sends an email — and everything that should happen next happens on its own: the welcome message, the paperwork, the internal notification, the first appointment slot.

Scheduling & follow-ups

Booking confirmations, reminders, rescheduling, and post-appointment check-ins go out at the right time without anyone keeping track of a calendar by hand.

Reporting & admin

Invoices, weekly summaries, and internal reports assemble themselves from work your team is already doing — no separate spreadsheet to maintain.

Tool connections

Your CRM, calendar, inbox, and payment system stop living in separate silos. Information entered once shows up everywhere it needs to.

AI-assisted responses

Common questions, first-pass replies, and routine documents get drafted automatically — always reviewed by a person before anything goes out, if you want that step.

Custom workflows

Something specific slowing your team down that doesn't fit a category? That's most of what I build — tell us the process and I'll design around it.
